This is a small game made in a month as a challenge to the constraints.

I learned a lot about godot and rust in general. Unless I get a good reason,
this is the end of development for this game.

You can use up your scary boss points to fry the workers, and there is a package
that is randomly assembled and shown, but not much else. Press left and right
to move the selector, and spacebar to select the worker. When you are out of
points, escape quits.

Thanks for looking!
